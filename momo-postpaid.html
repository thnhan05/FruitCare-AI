A Three-Sided Value Ecosystem

Benefits & Responsibilities of Participating Parties

1. Customers (SMEs/Co-ops)

Benefits: "Buy now, pay later" without complex income verification. Converts invoices to installments. Protects cash flow from seasonal risks.

Responsibilities: Must maintain a good CIC credit profile, settle balances on time, and pay applicable fees.

2. FruitCare AI (Provider)

Benefits: Removes price barriers, ensures immediate or predictable cash flow, and drives customer retention with embedded finance.

Responsibilities: Acts as first-layer risk filter (checking business capacity) and reserves operational enforcement rights.

3. MoMo (Credit Partner)

Benefits: Acquires high-quality B2B users, diversifies revenue via fees, and minimizes capital risk via dual underwriting.

Responsibilities: Final credit approval, limit allocation, disbursement, and imposing dual-lock risk control.

FruitCare AI B2B Policy

Risk Pre-Screening & Dual-Lock Architecture

FruitCare AI requires business customers to provide Annual Financial Statements (AFS) and Historical Business Performance Data to act as a first-layer quality filter, minimizing risk for the ecosystem.

ARTICLE 1. DUAL UNDERWRITING

Business capability is assessed using AFS and performance data ensuring ROI can cover repayment obligations. MoMo verifies legal compliance, bank links, and CIC credit profile.

ARTICLE 2. B2B INSTALLMENT CONVERSION

Applies to Basic, Standard, and Premium FruitCare AI packages. Supported tenures up to 12 months. Prepayment is not allowed for FruitCare AI systems via Postpaid Wallet.

ARTICLE 3. SEASONAL CASH FLOW PROTECTION

Supports seasonal export fluctuations. Customers can use the Minimum Payment feature (15% of balance). The remaining 85% is carried forward without being classified as bad debt.

ARTICLE 4. SERVICE FEES & CHARGES

Transparent MoMo wallet fees apply: VND 30k maintenance fee (from 6th month), Installment Conversion fee depending on tenure (3/6/9/12 mos), and 6.3% Minimum Payment fee on rolled-over balances.

ARTICLE 5. BREACH HANDLING (DUAL-LOCK)

In cases of default, MoMo applies financial penalties and limit reductions. FruitCare AI reserves the operational right to freeze Web Dashboards and AI modules until obligations are settled.
